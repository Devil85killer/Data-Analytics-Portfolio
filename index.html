Banking Audit & Fraud Detection System

Advanced SQL (Window Functions) Power BI Risk Analytics
  • 🚨 The Core Business Problem The bank's compliance team was manually auditing 500K+ transaction logs monthly. This reactive approach was slow, costing thousands of man-hours, and leaving the institution highly vulnerable to undetected, rapid fraudulent transfers.
  • 🛠️ My Technical Approach I bypassed basic aggregations and engineered advanced SQL scripts utilizing **Window Functions (`LEAD`, `RANK`, `OVER`)** and **Common Table Expressions (CTEs)**. I created an automated logic pipeline to instantly flag "Rapid High-Value Transfers" (e.g., 3+ transfers over ₹1L within a 10-minute window).
  • 🔍 The "So What?" (Deep Insight) The data revealed a glaring blind spot: 82% of flagged anomalous transactions consistently occurred outside of normal banking hours (11 PM - 4 AM) and were heavily concentrated in 3 specific Tier-2 branch regions.
  • 🚀 Strategic Recommendation & Action Based on these insights, I recommended the risk team to:
    1. Implement a mandatory "Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) cooling period" for any high-value transfers initiated between 11 PM and 4 AM.
    2. Temporarily lower the auto-approval threshold for the 3 identified high-risk branches until physical KYC audits were completed.
  • 💰 Estimated Business Impact Deploying this SQL anomaly detection framework as a pre-filter reduces the manual compliance audit workload by 60%, allowing the risk team to proactively block high-probability fraud rather than investigating it after the money is gone.

Olist E-Commerce Sales & Churn Analytics

SQL Python Power BI
  • The Business Problem The business was experiencing a severe drop in repeat customers (high churn) and needed to understand what was destroying Customer Lifetime Value (CLV).
  • My Approach & Technical Execution Executed complex SQL queries to join 5 distinct datasets (Orders, Customers, Reviews, Payments, Logistics) totaling over 100K rows. Conducted Python EDA to find correlations between review scores and operational metrics.
  • Deep Analytics Insights Discovered that product quality wasn't the core issue. Customers in Northern regions experienced a 40% higher delivery delay. 85% of customers giving a 1-star rating cited "logistics delays" and never returned.
  • Strategic Business Recommendation Recommended shifting logistics partners for the top 3 delayed northern states and implementing an automated "proactive apology discount" email for any order delayed past 48 hours to save customer retention.
