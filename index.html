Healthcare Operations & Revenue Optimization

Python T-SQL Power BI
  • Dataset: Synthesized Hospital Records (100K+ rows)
  • Problem: High appointment no-show rates were causing unpredictable daily revenue and severe staff underutilization.
  • Approach: Cleaned raw patient records using Python (Pandas), executed T-SQL joins to merge scheduling and billing tables, and engineered a dynamic Power BI tracking dashboard.
  • Key Insights: Discovered a 22% peak in no-shows among the 18-25 age demographic, specifically during early morning slots.
  • Business Impact: Recommended implementing automated SMS reminders targeting this specific demographic, projecting a potential 12% reduction in no-shows and recovery of previously lost daily revenue.

Banking Audit & Fraud Detection System

Advanced SQL Power BI
  • Dataset: Mock Financial Transaction Logs (500K+ rows)
  • Problem: Manual auditing of massive transaction logs was highly inefficient, leaving the institution vulnerable to undetected fraudulent patterns.
  • Approach: Engineered advanced SQL queries utilizing Window Functions (LEAD, RANK, OVER) and CTEs to automatically flag anomalies, such as rapid high-value transfers within short timeframes.
  • Key Insights: Identified that 8% of flagged anomalous transactions consistently occurred outside of normal banking hours from specific high-risk branch regions.
  • Business Impact: The SQL-based anomaly detection framework successfully reduced manual compliance audit time by 60%, drastically improving early fraud detection capabilities.

Olist E-Commerce Sales & Churn Analytics

SQL Power BI
  • Dataset: Olist Brazilian E-Commerce Dataset (Kaggle)
  • Problem: The business needed to understand regional sales performance disparities and identify the root causes behind customers not making repeat purchases (churn).
  • Approach: Utilized SQL to join multiple tables (customers, orders, reviews) to calculate customer lifetime metrics, and designed a multi-page Power BI dashboard for geographical sales tracking.
  • Key Insights: Revealed that delayed delivery times in specific geographic states directly correlated with negative reviews and a 35% higher customer churn rate.
  • Business Impact: Highlighted critical logistical bottlenecks, allowing the operations team to focus on optimizing delivery routes in high-churn regions to boost customer lifetime value.
