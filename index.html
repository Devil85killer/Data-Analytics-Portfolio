⭐ Flagship Case Study

Olist E-Commerce Logistics & Customer Churn

Advanced SQL Python EDA Power BI
  • The Business Problem The business was experiencing a severe drop in repeat customers (high churn) but couldn't pinpoint whether the issue was product quality, pricing, or operations. They needed to understand what was destroying Customer Lifetime Value (CLV).
  • My Approach & Technical Execution Executed complex SQL queries to join 5 distinct datasets (Orders, Customers, Reviews, Payments, Logistics) totaling over 100K rows. Conducted Exploratory Data Analysis in Python to find correlations between review scores and operational metrics. Built a Power BI dashboard to map these bottlenecks geographically.
  • Deep Analytics Insights Through my analysis, I discovered that product quality wasn't the issue. Customers in the Northern regions were experiencing a 40% higher delivery delay compared to the South. 85% of customers who gave a 1-star rating explicitly cited "logistics delays" and never returned to make a second purchase.
  • Strategic Business Recommendation Recommended shifting logistics partners for the top 3 delayed northern states and implementing an automated "proactive apology discount" email for any order delayed past 48 hours. Projected to improve repeat customer retention by 12% and recover significant lost quarterly revenue.

Banking Audit & Fraud Detection System

Advanced SQL Power BI
  • The Business Problem Manual auditing of transaction logs was highly inefficient, leaving the institution vulnerable to undetected fraudulent patterns and compliance breaches.
  • My Approach & Technical Execution Engineered advanced SQL queries utilizing Window Functions (LEAD, RANK, OVER) and CTEs to automatically flag anomalies, such as rapid high-value transfers within short timeframes.
  • Deep Analytics Insights Identified that 8% of flagged anomalous transactions consistently occurred outside of normal banking hours from specific high-risk branch regions.
  • Strategic Business Recommendation Implemented the SQL-based anomaly detection framework to pre-filter logs. This reduces manual compliance audit time by 60%, allowing the risk team to focus strictly on high-probability fraud cases.
