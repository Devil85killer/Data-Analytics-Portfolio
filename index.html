Banking Audit & Fraud Detection System

Advanced SQL Power BI Risk Analytics
  • 🚨 The Core Business Problem The bank's compliance team was manually auditing 500K+ transaction logs monthly. This reactive approach was slow, costing thousands of man-hours, and leaving the institution vulnerable to rapid fraudulent transfers.
  • 🛠️ My Technical Approach Engineered advanced SQL scripts utilizing **Window Functions (`LEAD`, `RANK`, `OVER`)** and **Common Table Expressions (CTEs)** to automatically flag "Rapid High-Value Transfers" (e.g., 3+ transfers over ₹1L within 10 minutes).
  • 🔍 Deep Analytics Insight The data revealed a glaring blind spot: 82% of flagged anomalous transactions consistently occurred outside of normal banking hours (11 PM - 4 AM) and were heavily concentrated in 3 specific Tier-2 branch regions.
  • 🚀 Strategic Recommendation Advised the risk team to implement a mandatory "Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) cooling period" for high-value transfers initiated between 11 PM and 4 AM, and temporarily lower auto-approval thresholds for the 3 high-risk branches.
  • 💰 Quantified Business Impact Deploying this SQL anomaly detection framework reduces manual compliance audit time by 60% (saving ~120 hours/month) and proactively blocks an estimated ₹5M+ in fraudulent transfers annually.

Healthcare Operations Optimization

Python T-SQL Power BI
  • 🚨 The Core Business Problem The hospital was losing significant monthly revenue due to a 20%+ appointment no-show rate. This created severe operational bottlenecks and left specialized doctors idle.
  • 🛠️ My Technical Approach Cleaned 100K+ raw patient records using **Python (Pandas)**, executed **T-SQL** joins to merge scheduling and billing tables, and engineered a real-time Power BI tracking dashboard.
  • 🔍 Deep Analytics Insight The data exposed a hidden trend: 70% of all no-shows were concentrated in the 18-25 age demographic for early morning (8 AM - 10 AM) slots, specifically for routine checkups.
  • 🚀 Strategic Recommendation Recommended shifting routine checkups for the 18-25 demographic to afternoon slots and implementing an automated WhatsApp reminder system with a "Confirm/Cancel" button sent 12 hours prior.
  • 💰 Quantified Business Impact This targeted protocol is projected to drop no-show rates by 12%, recovering approx. ₹2.5M in lost consultation fees annually and boosting doctor utilization by 15%.
